FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before we start.

What kind of projects does SI Labbz take on?

We build web applications, mobile apps (iOS & Android), and provide dedicated QA and digital marketing services — from MVPs for startups to enterprise-grade platforms.

How do you price projects?

Every engagement is scoped to your needs. We offer fixed-price milestones for well-defined work and dedicated-team models for ongoing development. Book a free consultation for a tailored quote.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We've served clients globally for 5+ years across multiple time zones, with clear communication and overlapping working hours.

How long does a typical project take?

An MVP can ship in 4–8 weeks; larger platforms run in agile sprints over several months. We'll give you a realistic timeline after the discovery call.

Do you provide support after launch?

Yes — long-term support and maintenance are part of how we work. We offer flexible support plans to keep your product secure, fast, and evolving.

Who owns the code and IP?

You do. On full payment, all source code and intellectual property are transferred to you — no lock-in, ever.
